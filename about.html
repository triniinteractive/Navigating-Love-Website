Our Mission

We believe love is a journey, not a destination. Our mission is to provide guidance, resources, and inspiration to help people build meaningful relationships. Think about your own journey are you chasing love, or are you nurturing it where you already stand? What values guide you when you choose who to let into your life?

Our Founders

Erwin Smith founder of Navigating Love, has dedicated over twenty years to guiding individuals and couples through the complexities of relationships. Known for his calm presence and strategic mind, Erwin approaches love the way he once approached leadership — with vision, patience, and a deep respect for human resilience. His coaching emphasizes trust, communication, and the courage to face difficult truths together. Erwin believes that every relationship is a journey of discovery, where challenges are not obstacles but opportunities to grow closer. His clients often describe him as both inspiring and practical, blending motivational insight with actionable steps. Erwin’s legacy is rooted in helping people uncover the deeper meaning of connection, reminding them that love thrives when we dare to question, to sacrifice, and to hope.

Levi Ackerman Navigating Love’s resident dating coach, brings four years of experience helping individuals and couples strengthen their bonds with clarity and discipline. Known for his straightforward approach, Levi believes that love thrives when honesty and respect are non‑negotiable. His coaching style is direct yet compassionate, guiding clients to cut through emotional clutter and focus on what truly matters in a relationship. Levi emphasizes the importance of small, consistent actions — listening carefully, showing appreciation, and maintaining boundaries — as the foundation of lasting love. He often reminds his clients that romance is not about grand gestures alone, but about the daily commitment to care and grow together. With his sharp insight and calm presence, Levi inspires people to confront challenges head‑on, build trust, and embrace vulnerability. His philosophy is simple: love requires courage, discipline, and the willingness to fight for connection.

Miche Zacharias a founding member of Navigating Love, brings over a decade of experience as a relationship coach and holds a PhD in Love Science. Known for his warmth and wisdom, Miche has guided countless couples and individuals through the challenges of intimacy, trust, and emotional growth. His approach blends academic insight with practical strategies, making complex ideas about love accessible and actionable. Miche believes that relationships thrive when partners embrace both vulnerability and strength, and he often reminds his clients that true connection is built through patience, empathy, and shared purpose. His coaching emphasizes the importance of small, daily acts of care as the foundation for lasting bonds. With his calm presence and deep knowledge, Miche inspires people to see love not just as an emotion, but as a discipline — one that requires courage, consistency, and a willingness to grow together.

Eren Yeager one of Navigating Love’s youngest coaches, brings two years of fresh perspective to the world of dating and relationships. Known for his passionate energy and fearless honesty, Eren encourages clients to confront their emotions head‑on and embrace vulnerability as a strength. His coaching style is bold yet empathetic, helping individuals break free from self‑doubt and pursue authentic connections. Eren believes that love is not about perfection, but about growth through shared struggles and triumphs. He often reminds his clients that courage in relationships means daring to express feelings openly, even when it feels uncomfortable. With his youthful insight, Eren emphasizes the importance of resilience — learning from heartbreak, standing firm in values, and continuing to seek meaningful bonds. His philosophy is simple: love is a journey of freedom and discovery, and every step forward brings us closer to the connection we truly desire.
